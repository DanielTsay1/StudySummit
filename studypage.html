Spaced Repetition
Spaced repetition is a study technique involving reviewing information in spaced intervals over time. This method leverages the spacing effect, which suggests that information is better retained when repeated over spaced intervals rather than in a single session. As a result, spaced repetition is able to optimize long-term memory retention while minimizing the time needed for review.
Feynman Technique
The Feynman Technique is a learning method centered around the concept of teaching a subject to someone else. By teaching the material you are forced to confront any gaps in understanding. The act of explaining the topic helps solidify one's own understanding and retention of the material.
