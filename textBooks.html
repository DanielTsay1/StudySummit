Textbooks 101
Intro

As you may have experienced in college, high school or any type of education, textbooks are important. Textbooks are designed to break down hard and diffucult concepts and make them understandable, that is why you will most likely dread reading them because there is so much information. Well let's break it down and make it easier to read and use.



Breaking it down

Make sure to break down the paragraphs into smaller sections so that you understand each piece. When you understand all the smaller texts then you can puzzle them together to get the bigger idea and concept. Additionally, do not spend hours and hours on end just reading the textbook, make sure to go over text multiple times and use active recall to help you in understanding the material.



Connecting it with different tools

Textbooks are supposed to help you. A packet that a teacher might tell you how to do something but it will not explain why you do some steps. Memorizing steps is not the same as understandin the steps. Memorizing the Times table is easy but when one will have to use it then they might blank because they might have forgotten the answer. But someone that understands how to multiply will actually multiply the numbers together and by doing this they will have a harder time forgetting because they don't remember.



Conclusion

Textbooks are tools and by learning and practicing how to use them you will become the academic weapon. Textbooks are meant to help you so try not to dread them/
